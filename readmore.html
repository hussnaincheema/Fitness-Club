Gym Overview

FITNESS CLUB is a startup locally owned club located in XYZ. FITNESS CLUB will include various exercise equipment, strength training equipment, free weights, aerobic equipment, and plenty of cardio equipment. The club will also offer HIIT group classes, spin classes, strength training instruction, and personal training.
Located in an up and coming, newly developed, and highly trafficked area of town, FITNESS CLUB will attract both men and women ages 16 to 75+. There is something for everyone at FITNESS CLUB, whether someone is just beginning their fitness journey or is a seasoned athlete and gymrat. The club will have a welcoming atmosphere, experienced personal trainers, friendly staff, and frequently cleaned equipment and restrooms.
The membership options are affordable and will be offered on a month-to-month basis, a 6 or 12 month contract, or the ability to pay for an entire year upfront. Discounts will be offered to military, teachers, and healthcare workers.

Service Offering

The following are the services that FITNESS CLUB will provide:

  • Strength training equipment
  • Cardio equipment
  • Free weights/dumbbells
  • Aerobic equipment
  • HIIT group classes
  • Strength training instruction
  • Training sessions upon request

Customer Focus

FITNESS CLUB will target all men and women ages 16 – 75+ who are interested in achieving and maintaining a healthy and active lifestyle. XYZ is a very diverse city that includes men and women from all walks of life. There are numerous blue collar individuals, white collar individuals, government employees, and military members as the local Army base is one of the largest in the country. Since FITNESS CLUB will be very affordable, it will be able to attract people from all walks of life.

Management Team

FITNESS CLUB's Manager will manage the day-to-day tasks of running the club, while overseeing the staff, scheduling the group HIIT classes, and making sure all equipment is working properly and safely. He will also be in charge of ordering any new and replacement equipment and cleaning products and supplies. FITNESS CLUB's Manager will hire a gym manager to manage all new member sign-ups, placing them on a recurring payment schedule, and handle all member issues. There will be three trainers that will be available to personally train any member that requests training. They will also take turns running the group HIIT classes. FITNESS CLUB's Manager will outsource a local CPA to handle payroll, accounting, and tax payments. He will also outsource a local advertising company to develop the club’s logo, branding, photography, social media accounts, website, and billboard design.

Success Factors

FITNESS CLUB will be able to achieve success by offering the following competitive advantages:

  • Large, clean, and spacious facility that has all the necessary equipment for their fitness routine.
  • Knowledgeable and friendly staff of trainers.
  • Best value for membership pricing. The membership pricing options are affordable and offer military, teacher, and healthcare employee discounts.
  • Convenient hours of operation.
